Housebound Chocolate Chip Thins

Housebound Chocolate Chip Thins

Description:

Rating: 5 stars
Servings: 24
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Course: Dessert
Cuisine: American


Ingredients:

• 1/2 cup unsalted butter at room temp
• 1/4 cup brown sugar
• 3/4 cup white sugar
• 1 egg
• 1 1/4 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 1 1/4 cups flour
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
• 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
• 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Please follow the steps accordingly to achieve maximum baking pleasure...

Step 1: Dress Accordingly

This is the most important step when making your quarantine thins. Cover areas that may: secrete, sweat, breathe, drip, make skin-to-skin contact, waft, etc. Put on a face mask to makesure your breath never even thinks of touching the ingredients. Proper hand gloves that will shield from contamination as well as make it easier to roll dough. Hair should be up or covered.

Step 2: Cream Together

Cream together the butter and sugars in a mixer or by hand.

Step 3: Wet Ingredients

Add the egg and vanilla, making sure to scrape down the sides of the mixing bowl.

Step 4: Dry Ingredients

Add the flour, salt, baking soda and baking powder and mix until everything is incorporated.

Step 5: Toppings

Fold in the chocolate chips or any other toppings that peek your intrest.

Step 6: Assemble

Take two spoons, an ice cream scoop, or use your hands to assemble the dough into circles and place on a baking sheet.

Step 7: Bake

Place the baking sheet with the cookies in the oven for 10 minutes at 350 degrees.

Step 8: Partake in Consumption

Once cookies have settled and cooled down, consume quickly.

Step 9: Spread Goods, Not Disease

Now break quarantine to bring ravishing goods to your roomates so they too can consume quickly.
